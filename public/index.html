Our promises to you

  • We never sell your data. Funded by supporters, never ads or brokers, so you are the user, not the product.
  • Money never buys reach. Supporting adds a badge and storage, never a feed spot, karma, or a louder vote. Everyone stays equal.
  • Real humans, one voice each. Anti-bot and anti-sybil protections stop influence being bought with fake accounts or bot farms.
  • Fair, transparent moderation. Every action is in a public log, flagged content is judged by a jury of real members, and you can always appeal.
  • No secret algorithm. Feed ranking is simple and published in our code, so nothing is hidden and no one is quietly throttled.
  • Your data is truly yours. Export everything in one click, or permanently delete your account and all media anytime. No lock-in.
  • The community decides. Big changes go to a public vote, one human one vote, not a boardroom.
  • Open source, always. Every line of our code lives in the open on GitHub under the AGPL licence.
