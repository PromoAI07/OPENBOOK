Why OpenBook is different

Most social networks make money from your attention and your data. OpenBook is built on the opposite promise. You get a Facebook-style feed, friends, and photos together with Reddit-style communities and voting, all in one place, without the surveillance. Every claim below is checkable in our open source code.

✓ No ads, no data selling

OpenBook is funded by supporters who want it to exist. We never run ads and never sell your data, so you are the user, not the product being sold.

✓ Money never buys reach

Supporting us unlocks a badge and more storage, nothing more. It can never buy a place in the feed, extra karma, or a louder vote. Everyone stays equal.

✓ No secret algorithm

The feed ranking is simple and published in the code. Nothing is hidden, and no one is quietly throttled in the dark.

✓ Your data is truly yours

Export everything you have ever posted in one click, and permanently delete your account and all its media whenever you want. No lock-in.

✓ The community decides

Big changes and new features go to a public vote, one human one vote, not a corporate boardroom.

✓ Open source, always

Every line of OpenBook is public under the AGPL licence. If the rules and the code ever disagree, that is a bug you can point to. No black box to trust.

✓ Transparent, fair moderation

Every moderation action is in a public log, flagged content is judged by a jury of real members, and any action can be appealed.

✓ Real humans, one voice each

Anti-bot and anti-sybil protections keep influence from being bought with fake accounts or bot farms. No engagement farming, no manipulation rings.
