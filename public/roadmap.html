Community Roadmap

OpenBook is steered by the people who use it. Anyone can suggest a change, everyone votes, and the most-wanted ideas rise. This board is public so the direction of the platform is never decided behind closed doors.

Each card shows its raw community score (one human, one vote, the official tally). Where it differs, a sybil-filtered score is shown beside it for transparency.
